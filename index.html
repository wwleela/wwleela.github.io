Built on the streets of Hyderabad.

Urban Gliding Hyderabad was founded on a simple observation: Hyderabad had aspiring skaters, but lacked accessible, professional coaching infrastructure. Coach Leela — armed with IOC certification and a vision to democratize skate education — pioneered the society-based coaching model. Instead of asking families to commute to expensive rinks, UGH brings world-class training directly to residential complexes across the city.

This isn't recreational skating. This is structured, internationally-aligned curriculum that takes complete beginners through systematic skill progression — whether you're a 5-year-old discovering wheels for the first time or a 50-year-old reclaiming urban mobility.

🎓

IOC Certified Methodology

Internationally-recognized curriculum developed through Olympic Committee standards. Your progression follows proven frameworks used by professional skating coaches worldwide — adapted for Indian neighborhoods, but uncompromising on teaching quality.

🏘️

Society-Based Coaching Revolution

Phase 1 workshops happen at your residential society. Coach Leela sets up the training ground in your complex's open spaces — no travel for families, no rink memberships, no logistical friction. This hyperlocal approach transforms skating from "that activity we drive across town for" into "that skill we practice in our own neighborhood."

🛡️

Safety as Foundation, Not Afterthought

Helmet, knee pads, wrist guards — mandatory before anyone touches wheels. UGH operates on certified safety protocols designed for mixed-age groups in open environments. Ages 5 and above welcome, because safety systems scale to protect every body type and experience level.

🔄

Hybrid Coaching Architecture

2 physical sessions + 2 technical video guides per week. This isn't "choose between in-person or online" — it's a deliberate hybrid system where Coach Leela provides hands-on correction twice weekly, then reinforces concepts with custom video breakdowns tailored to your specific progress gaps.
