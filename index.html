Urban Gliding Hyderabad brings IOC certified skate coaching directly to your residential complex. No commuting. No expensive rink memberships. Coach Leela comes to you.

IOC Certified Coaching
Internationally recognised curriculum. Structured progression from absolute beginner to confident glider — for every age.
We Come to Your Society
Phase 1 workshops at your residential complex. No travel required. Equipment provided. Just register and show up.
Safety First, Every Session
Helmet, knee pads, and wrist guards before anyone steps on wheels. Certified protocols. Ages 5 and above welcome.
Hybrid Coaching Model
2 physical sessions + 2 technical video guides per week. Progress at your pace, supported digitally between sessions.
